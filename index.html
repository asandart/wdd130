South Africa

South Africa is located in the southern region of Africa. It is bordered by the countries Namibia, Botswana, Zimbabwe and Eswatini. It has three capital cities; Pretoria the administrative capital, Cape Town the legislative capital and lastly Bloemfontein the judicial capital.
