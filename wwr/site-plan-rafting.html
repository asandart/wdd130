Rafting Site plan

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of establishing an online presence for my rafting business is to reach a broader audience, engage with customers, streamline booking processes, and enhance our brand's visibility. It allows us to provide essential information, manage our reputation, and stay competitive in the industry while gaining valuable insights into customer preferences and behavior. Ultimately, our online presence aims to promote our services and improve the overall customer experience.

Audience

Our target audience is adventure seekers aged 18-45, passionate about outdoor activities and water sports, with moderate to high incomes. They desire thrilling, safe rafting experiences in picturesque natural settings. They expect to find detailed information on rafting tours, safety measures, pricing, and booking options. Their unmet needs include an easy-to-use booking platform and comprehensive trip details.Scenario:

Alex (28) is planning an adventure. Answer: Alex, an adventurous 28-year-old, visits our website for a rafting trip. He finds detailed tour info, pricing, safety measures, and reviews to plan his ideal adventure.

Scenario: Lisa (35) wants a corporate team-building event. Answer: Lisa, a 35-year-old entrepreneur, explores our corporate packages section for team-building options, customization details, and contacts for planning her company's event.

Scenario: Ben (22) seeks a last-minute adventure. Answer: Ben, a spontaneous 22-year-old, accesses our mobile-friendly site to quickly discover available weekend trips, last-minute deals, and an easy booking process for his impromptu rafting adventure.
